Simple Tutor is a non-profit organization committed to fostering educational growth and academic enrichment. Each summer, we offer engaging and accessible classes across a diverse range of subjects. Our mission is to minimize educational gaps, stimulate intellectual curiosity, and cultivate lifelong learners. We believe in the potential of every student, and strive to provide high-quality, tailored instruction that is not only beneficial, but also enjoyable. Join us this summer and experience a novel approach to learning that's as simple as it is effective.

Our Mission

Introducing Project Educate

We have formed a powerful partnership with Educate, an organization founded by student Pranav Bollineni. Together, our mission is to address educational disparities in third-world countries. Pranav's remarkable dedication has positively impacted numerous students in India, enabling them to excel in government and IIT exams.
